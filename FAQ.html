Do you serve or allow alcohol?

No, but we do serve Coke which pairs well with Moonpies which we also sell. Sorry, we don't serve alcohol and by "Coke" we mean we have can drinks. Everything in the South is "Coke" regardless of brand. We ask that you not bring alochol to the venue as well. We strive to always have a family friendly environment safe for all ages.

Where can I park?

We are downtown in small town USA so, there is very little activity going on at the same time of our shows. There is parking along the street, behind the building, and across the street. Just don't park anywhere marked "No Parking" like the Police Department across the street. You'd surely get a ticket for that. The sheriff might come to a show or two but, we're not in that good with him.

What if someone in my party is in a wheelchair?

No problem. We will accommodate you however possible. Our restroom and venue is ADA compliant.

What is your refund policy?

Well we really hate that you can't make it, but we understand that things come up. Reach out to us and let us know what's going on. We'll do everything we can to be sure you're happy. You can contact us here.

Do you allow photography?

Sure, pictures are no problem. The only time this would matter would be if the performing artist didn't want photography. We ain't into that yet, but if we do we'll address it at that time and will communicate at the show and online. If you do take pictures though, please be sure to tag us on social media - @thefiddleandbowmusichall. Also, be curteous of your neighbor and don't block their veiw of the show please.

How does my band get on stage?

If you are an artist that is interested in performing or the manager of a band or artist, the best thing to do is contact us directly. We'd much rather talk directly with you to be sure all your questions are answered completley. You can contact us here

How do I purchase some of your folk art?

If you want to buy art (or tickets), the process is really the same. You can contact us here or watch our page to see when we'll be at local art events. We try to advertise when Justin Atkin will be at local art shows to include locations.
